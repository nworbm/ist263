PROJECTS

Scholarli is an IST 263 project which seeks combine
the everyday needs of college students into one app.
Many students struggle with time management and
accessing helpful resources, which
Scholarli plans to fix.

After witnessing students throw away well-used items
my Freshman year and then spending hundreds of
dollars on dorm items in my Sophomore year, I
began working on an initiative which increases
sustainability and community on campus by recycling
and reselling dorm goods. Operations should begin by
end of Spring, 2024.

As a consultant for eHarmony
(through my MGT 247 class), I provided invaluable
insights and strategies to enhance user
experience and drive engagement on their platform.
